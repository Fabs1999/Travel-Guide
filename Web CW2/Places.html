Sevilla, Spain

Royal Alcazar

Opening hours from 09:30am - 7pm (Monday to Sunday)

Book the tickets in advance and prices for the tour start from €39 but can vary based on packages or additional guides.

Royal Alcazar is a historical place in Sevilla, with origins dating back to the 11th century. It served as a fortress and has evolved over time with diverse palaces and gardens.

Many scenes from TV shows like Game of Thrones have been filmed here, making it a popular tourist attraction.

Plaza De Espanha

Open space, accessible anytime!

Plaza de España was built for the Ibero-American Exhibition of 1929. It features a massive, semi-circular brick building with Renaissance/neo-Moorish architecture.

It has also been a filming location for movies like Star Wars and Lawrence of Arabia.

Antigüedades Bar de Tapas

Opening hours from 12pm-12am (Monday to Sunday)

Antiguedades Bar is housed in a beautiful, 300-year-old building in downtown Sevilla. It was originally a tailoring shop and later became an inn.

Before becoming a bar, it was known for its relic and antiques shop, hence the name Antigüedades.

It's known for its great ambiance and serves delicious food, including Gazpacho.

Cattedrale Di Siviglia

Opening hours from 10:45am to 5pm (Monday to Saturday) and Sundays from 2:30pm to 6pm.

Tickets start from €17.50 for adults. The Cathedral of Sevilla is the largest Gothic cathedral in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

It's known for its five precincts and Islamic influences in its design.

Toulon, France

Telepherique du Mont Faron

Opening hours from 10am to 1pm, 1:30pm to 7pm (Monday to Sunday).

Ticket Price for an Adult is €9, and €6 for children.

The Telepherique du Faron allows access to Mount Faron, approximately 584 meters above sea level, offering panoramic views.

Le Churchill

Opening hours from 6pm to 1am (Monday to Saturday).

Le Churchill is a cozy bar and restaurant with a great atmosphere. It's known for its tapas and excellent drinks.

The first floor offers a dining experience with an amazing view.

Burning of Fort Saint Louis

This annual event starts at 10pm and lasts 25 minutes. It commemorates the fort's resistance to the Austro-Sardinian troops in 1707.

The fort was initially destroyed during the battle but later rebuilt and renamed Fort Saint Louis.

Toulon Harbour

Toulon Harbour is a bustling naval port with numerous boats and restaurants. It offers stunning views and access to nearby islands.

It's a popular spot for dining and enjoying the sunny weather.

Nice, France

Parc de la Colline du Chateau

Opening hours from 08:30am to 08:00pm (Monday to Sunday).

Parc de la Colline du Chateau offers panoramic views of Nice. It was once a military citadel and has historic significance.

It's a great place for a leisurely walk and to enjoy the scenery.

Quai des Etats-Unis

Located near Old Nice, Quai des Etats-Unis is a popular lane near Baie des Anges, known for its restaurants and nightlife.

It's lively in the evenings with singers and performers during summer.

Vieux Nice

Vieux Nice is the old town of Nice, known for its narrow streets, colorful buildings, and local markets.

It's a charming area with shops selling local products and outdoor eateries.

Musee Massena

Opening hours from 10am to 6pm (Sunday, Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday & Saturday).

Musee Massena is a museum housed in a historic belle epoque building, dedicated to the history of the Riviera.

It showcases the region's cultural heritage and attracts visitors interested in history and art.
