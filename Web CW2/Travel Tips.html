Sevilla

  • Get a hotel room with air conditioning and thank me later! For the best places with air conditioning, check here.
  • Pack light clothes, like plenty of t-shirts and shorts, as the weather in Sevilla is HOT.
  • If visiting the Real Alcazar, book tickets in advance! It's historically significant, stunning, and a popular filming location, attracting many tourists.
  • Learn some Spanish beyond just "Buenos Dias" and "como estas". While some places speak English, a more immersive experience awaits with some basic Spanish; try using Duolingo.
  • Most places are within walking distance, so consider staying in the city center to enhance your experience fivefold.

Toulon

  • Book your flight tickets in advance! Southern France can be pricey, so planning ahead can secure cheaper flights here.
  • If you drive, consider renting a car! There are many charming villages to explore. If not, rely on public transport.
  • Toulon offers many buses at €1 per trip; €10 buys 10 trips. You can also use the same ticket for boat trips to islands, with additional costs.
  • Seafood lovers rejoice! Southern France excels in seafood; some of the best I've had.
  • Not a fan of seafood? No worries! France offers diverse cuisine; from entrecôte to pastries, you're in for a treat.
  • Embrace rosé wine! Toulon specializes in this refreshing drink, ideal by the beach or marina.

Nice

  • Similarly, book your tickets early! Nice is bigger and better than Toulon, but also pricier.
  • With more buses, taxis, and transport options, getting around Nice is effortless.
  • Rise early! With fantastic weather likely, make the most of it, especially if traveling from the UK.
  • Flights to Nice are frequent; starting early and running late, ensuring a flight to fit your schedule.
  • Visit between September and June for Opera Nice Côte d'Azur tickets! A delightful concert, not to be missed.
