Sevilla

The great thing about Sevilla is that you can pretty much walk everywhere, although there are reliable buses available that cover most areas. Considering the weather, walking or cycling can be pleasant options.

The city is so beautiful that walking around is a delightful experience.

Toulon

Toulon is smaller than Sevilla, so you can explore most of it on foot within a couple of days.

For those who want to venture further, buses and boats are available for trips to islands or neighboring towns.

To reach higher areas, you can use the Telepherique or opt for a car .

Nice

Once again, walking is highly recommended in Nice.

If walking isn't your preference, there are plenty of buses , bicycles , and trams available to explore the city.
